FAQ

Questions before you reach out.

How fast can we launch?

Most Website Sprint projects launch in 2–4 weeks depending on scope and how quickly content comes together. I keep cycles tight and ship in clear milestones — no vague "it's almost done."

What about pricing? Do you have fixed packages?

I scope projects individually so you're not paying for things you don't need. I'll give you a clear, written estimate after a short call — not before I understand your goals. If a project isn't the right fit, I'll say so.

Will I be able to edit my site after launch?

Yes. The handoff is clean and intentional — you'll get documentation, and the code is structured so that basic updates don't require a developer. If you want help with ongoing changes, the Calm Growth Retainer covers that.

Can you improve an existing site instead of rebuilding?

Absolutely. Sometimes a targeted redesign of the homepage, a speed pass, or a better contact flow is all you need. I'll tell you honestly whether a rebuild makes sense or whether quick improvements would do more.

Do you write the copy or provide photos?

I can guide structure, tone, and what each section needs to say. You provide the copy (or a rough draft I can refine) and any photos. I can help you source stock photography or find a local photographer if needed.

What happens after the site launches?

You own it completely. If anything comes up — a broken link, a form issue, a quick update — I'm available. For ongoing improvements, the Calm Growth Retainer keeps me in your corner every month.
