The Vision

Industry Salon Lounge was preparing to open its doors in Newtown Square with a clear goal: create a salon experience that felt elevated, welcoming, and modern from the very first touchpoint. As a brand- new business, their website needed to do more than exist— it needed to introduce the salon, communicate their aesthetic, and support a smooth opening.

During this phase, the owners recognized that their existing setup didn't fully reflect the quality or direction of the salon, and a transition was needed to align their digital presence with their vision.

Crafting the Experience

Stepping in during the final stages before launch, the focus was on creating a seamless transition from the previous platform to a fully custom website without disrupting the momentum of opening a new business. The site was designed to feel refined yet approachable, mirroring the lounge-style atmosphere of the salon itself.

From layout and typography to performance and usability, every element was built with clarity, ease, and longevity in mind. The result was a streamlined experience that allows clients to quickly understand the brand, explore services, and feel confident booking from their very first visit.

The Reveal

The finished website launched alongside Industry Salon's opening, delivering a polished online presence that matched the energy and intention of the physical space. With a modern design, clear structure, and flexible foundation for future growth, the site now supports the salon's brand as it continues to grow.

What began as a last-minute transition became a smooth, successful digital launch—helping Industry Salon start strong with a website built to grow with them.
