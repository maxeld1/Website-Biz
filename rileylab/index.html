The Vision

Enable faster, more connected discovery by unifying the Riley Lab’s critical tools and workflows in a single, dependable portal—so researchers can stay focused on immunology and therapeutic development.

The goal was clarity and continuity: reduce operational friction, surface the right links at the right moments, and support ongoing work across cancer, HIV, diabetes, and related programs.

Crafting the Experience

Built around real lab workflows—data access, scheduling, maintenance rotations, and shared resources— the portal prioritizes fast navigation, minimal clicks, and consistent access for every researcher.

The interface pairs modern academic visual language with practical structure, so teams can quickly locate what they need, stay aligned, and keep research moving without administrative drag.

The Reveal

The finished portal delivers a streamlined research operations dashboard—bringing tools, schedules, and announcements into one modern, dependable environment for the Riley Lab.

The result is a clear, scalable foundation that supports daily lab workflows today and future expansion as research needs evolve.
