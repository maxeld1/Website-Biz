The Vision

Richie’s work spans comedy, theatre, and original performance. The portfolio needed to feel confident, expressive, and instantly scannable, without losing the personality that makes his work memorable.

The visual direction pulled from stage lighting and neon signage: dark foundation, green accents, and high-contrast typography to put the work front and center.

Crafting the Experience

We structured the portfolio around clear categories (original works, musicals, plays, live music, sketch), so visitors can jump directly into the work they care about.

The layout stays minimal and bold, with large previews and a dark palette that keeps focus on imagery and title treatments, just like a stage.

The Reveal

The final portfolio delivers a confident, stage-ready presence: bold visuals, fast navigation, and a clean structure that lets Richie’s work speak first.

It’s a flexible system that can expand with new shows, reels, and collaborations as the work evolves.
